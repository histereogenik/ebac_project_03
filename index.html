About the project

Awakening music. I'm a Brazilian music producer and technology lover. I've always felt the psychedelic music as a therapy and as I went deep into my self-knowledge studies I began to understand my purpose on life and how it connects with music and it's healing power, the power I've experienced in my body and soul! In my experience, Psytrance makes so much sense to me that my body just moves with it without thinking, so it becomes an expression of my soul and truth, an exercise of presence and awareness, a meditation. As it is when I'm making my music, I don't think about it, music just happens... just flows... again it becomes a meditation and therefore truth comes out of this silence, this truth comes with CONSCIOUSNESS. A very wise friend always says to me, "When consciousness meets consciousness, there is LOVE".

How to fully enjoy it

This project comes with the intention of opening the path for your essence to manifest, to support your body and soul find its natural alignment to the source of creation. I'm channeling sounds that vibrate frequencies of healing and love, I invite you to play my music in a space where you can dance, close your eyes and just allow yourself to be present, aware, free.
Enjoy the journey within, back home. :)
